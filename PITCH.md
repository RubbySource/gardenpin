# GardenPin — Pitch & App Store Copy

## One-liner (EN)
GardenPin lets you pin your garden plants on a real photo of your garden, then automatically exports seasonal care reminders — pruning, fertilizing, winter protection — straight to your Apple or Google calendar.

## Short description (EN)
Track every plant in your garden by pinning it on your own garden photo. When you add a plant, GardenPin suggests the right seasonal care actions for that species — you pick which ones matter, and they land in your calendar as yearly reminders. No daily nagging, just the big annual tasks when you actually need them.

## Key differentiators
- 📍 Pin plants on YOUR real garden photo (not a generic grid)
- 📅 iCal export — works with Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, any calendar app
- 🌱 Seasonal care focus: pruning, fertilizing, winter prep — not watering
- 🇨🇿 Czech plant database (85+ plants, cultivars, Central European focus)
- 📱 Mobile-first PWA — works on iPhone without App Store

## Target user
Czech gardeners with ornamental gardens: shrubs, ornamental grasses, trees, perennials.
Primary: homeowners 30-55 with established gardens who forget when to prune.
